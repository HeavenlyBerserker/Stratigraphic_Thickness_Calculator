Help

  1. Choose the model that matches your geometry assumptions.
  2. Enter measured values and angles using the documented conventions in the README.
  3. Optional: enter non-zero standard deviations (σ) to enable Monte Carlo outputs (leave σ = 0 for deterministic runs). Click the `?` icon for a quick cheatsheet of what to do with σ.
  4. Review geometry warnings in fold models before final interpretation.
  5. Export results/plots when needed for reporting and auditability.

Visit the repository for additional instructions, or the paper (coming soon) for yet more details.
